Breakfast Sandwich - Peace and Love Style

Description

A bacon egg and cheese breakfast sandwich made with a bagel

  • A plain bagel
  • 1-2 slices Kraft american cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 strips of bacon

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon in frying pan on stove
  2. Once cooked, remove bacon from pan and use paper towel to remove excess grease
  3. Remove majority of bacon grease from pan, leaving small amount
  4. Crack eggs into pan with bacon grease, cook until no longer runny
  5. Toast bagel while eggs cook
  6. Combine ingredients when cooked, placing bacon on top of eggs and cheese on top of bacon
  7. Place hot top of bagel onto cheese, press down with spatual to melt cheese
  8. Serve hot
