Sweet Potato Tacos

Description

Super fast and delicious tacos substituting sweet potatos in place of the meat.

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Sweet Potatos
  • Cheese
  • Black Beans
  • Salsa
  • Hot Sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook sweet potatos 30-45 min in oven or campfire until soft
  2. Cook beans on stove when potatos have about 10 min left
  3. Warm the tortillas
  4. Place small amount of cheese on each tortilla
  5. Scoop sweet potato onto the cheese
  6. Add beans, salsa, and hot sauce of choice
  7. Serve hot
