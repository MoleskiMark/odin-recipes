Tasty Pizza

Description

Homemade pizza

  • Pizza dough
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni (optional)

Steps

  1. Roll out the pizza dough into circular shape
  2. Spread sauce evenly onto pizza dough
  3. Spread mozerella cheese over sauce
  4. Add pepperoni and other toppings of choice
  5. Bake at max oven temp for 10-12 minutes
